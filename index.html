Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create my wedding song?
Our standard delivery time is 24-48 hours for all packages. Premium and Deluxe packages receive priority processing within 24 hours. We also offer express delivery within 12 hours for an additional $50.
What information do I need to provide for my custom song?
We'll ask for your names, how you met, special moments in your relationship, your wedding date, preferred music style, and any specific lyrics or themes you'd like included. The more details you provide, the more personalized your song will be!
Can I request revisions if I'm not satisfied?
Yes! Basic package includes 1 revision, Premium includes 2 revisions, and Deluxe includes unlimited revisions. Additional revisions can be purchased for $15 each. We want you to be completely happy with your song!
What music genres do you offer?
Basic package focuses on ballads, while Premium and Deluxe packages can be created in any genre including pop, rock, country, jazz, acoustic, R&B, and more. Just let us know your preference when ordering!
How will I receive my wedding song?
Your personalized wedding song will be delivered directly to your email inbox. Basic package includes MP3 format, while Premium and Deluxe packages include both high-quality WAV and MP3 files for maximum compatibility.
Can I use the song for my wedding ceremony?
Absolutely! Your custom wedding song is created specifically for you and can be used for your ceremony, reception, first dance, or any other wedding-related event. You own the rights to use your personalized song.
What if I need my song in a different language?
We can create songs in multiple languages including English, German, Spanish, French, and more. Simply specify your language preference when placing your order, and our specialists will craft your song accordingly.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your song after all included revisions, we'll provide a full refund. Your happiness is our priority!
Can I order a song as a gift for someone else?
Absolutely! Many customers order personalized wedding songs as gifts for engaged couples, newlyweds, or anniversary celebrations. Just provide the couple's story and details, and we'll create a beautiful surprise for them.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach us via email at info@wedding-song.de or call us at +49 1511 4423242. Premium and Deluxe customers also receive priority phone support. We're here to help make your wedding song perfect!
